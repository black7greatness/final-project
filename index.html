BLACK IS GREAT

As seen all over the world, black is always associated with greatness . This is hugely supported by the vast amount of African originating discoveries history,also the current sport affairs and excellence in the black millitary man-power, economic potential and many oher factors. As seen on the view of social status, Africa is viewed as the future of civilisation as other civilisations continue to show signs of collpse, the African social association has remained rooted to its natural ground. A blessing in disguise as it is seen. Thus, this website exists to provide a deeper connection of the African scholars to the roots of the future of civilization

ABOUT US

Established in the year 2020, The BLACK GREATNESS GROUP is a group of ambitious Africans, anthropologists, pan-africanists and web designers who have come together to work on the provision of a gate way to cultural education and literacy of the African people.The group came by as a result of an escalating need to tackle new cultural problems facing the African people as technology and advacement is also observed.Our dedication to educating the African people culturally is priceless as Africans have a rich culture.
